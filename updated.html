PIXEL PIONEERS

Description

Explore the dynamic world of computer graphics with our project, aiming to showcase fundamental transformations: translation, scaling, and rotation. Through intuitive visual demonstrations, users delve into the core concepts of transforming objects in digital space. Witness how translation seamlessly shifts objects across coordinates, scaling alters their size with precision, and rotation brings motion and orientation.

Objectives

  • Rotating the blades of windmill
  • POV of ship which translates and scales with time
  • The translation of apple

Challenges Faced

  • Implementing both translation and scaling on an object proved to be a challenging task, requiring meticulous coordination
    between the transformations to ensure accurate positioning and sizing relative to the project's specifications.
  • Rendering one animation while simultaneously rendering another posed a significant challenge in the project.
  • Coordinating the coordinates of various objects in my project became a major hurdle. Aligning their positions accurately
    while accounting for differences in scales and rotations demanded meticulous attention to detail.

How we Overcame Them

  • Documentation of Canvas has been used extensively while building the
    project.
  • Various resources from the web have supplemented our ideas.
  • Dummy objects have been used to trace the accurate coordinates of
    various elements.
